Adobo Recipes

Learn how to cook delicious Filipino adobo at home. These recipes are simple, flavorful, and perfect for beginners. Follow the steps and enjoy the rich, savory taste of this iconic Filipino dish.

Classic Chicken Adobo

Ingredients:
Chicken, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, peppercorns, water.

Steps:
1. Combine chicken, soy sauce, and garlic. Marinate for 30 minutes.
2. Add vinegar, bay leaves, peppercorns, and water.
3. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 30–40 minutes.
4. Serve hot with rice.

Pork Adobo

Ingredients:
Pork belly, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, peppercorns.

Steps:
1. Heat pork in a pot and cook until lightly browned.
2. Add garlic, soy sauce, vinegar, and spices.
3. Simmer until pork becomes tender.
4. Enjoy with steamed rice.

Adobo with Coconut Milk (Adobo sa Gata)

Ingredients:
Chicken or pork, coconut milk, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic.

Steps:
1. Cook meat with soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic.
2. Add coconut milk and simmer gently.
3. Cook until sauce thickens.
4. Serve warm.

Adobong Pusit

Ingredients:
Squid (pusit), soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, onion, bay leaves, pepper.

Steps:
1. Clean and slice the squid.
2. Sauté garlic and onion.
3. Add squid, soy sauce, vinegar, and spices.
4. Simmer briefly until squid is tender. Do not overcook.
5. Serve hot.

Adobong Sitaw

Ingredients:
String beans (sitaw), soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, onion, pepper.

Steps:
1. Cut sitaw into small pieces.
2. Sauté garlic and onion.
3. Add sitaw, soy sauce, vinegar, and pepper.
4. Cook until sitaw is tender.
5. Serve warm.

Cooking Tips

• Let adobo rest before serving to enhance flavor.
• Use natural vinegar for the best taste.
• Adobo tastes better the next day.
• Adjust soy sauce and vinegar to your preference.
